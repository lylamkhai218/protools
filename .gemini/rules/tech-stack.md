# tech-stack.md - Approved Engineering Technology Stack

This document defines standard, highly coherent engineering technologies approved for workspace configurations and frameworks.

## 🛠️ Architecture Layers & Tools

1. **Frontend Core**: React (Vite-powered, version 18+).
2. **Styling Engine**: Tailwind CSS.
3. **Icons & Assets**: Lucide React.
4. **Data Visualizations & Charts**: Recharts & D3.
5. **Animation & Motion**: Framer Motion (Framer or generic motion imports).
6. **Language Framework**: TypeScript (NoEmit, Strict, CJS or ESM).
