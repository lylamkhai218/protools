# naming-conventions.md - Semantic Coding Vocabulary

Enforce unified camelCase, PascalCase, or UPPERCASE names.

## 🔠 Standards
- **Components**: PascalCase (e.g., `CategoryCatalog`, `CartQuote`).
- **Hooks & Helpers**: camelCase (e.g., `useLanguage`, `handleAddToCart`).
- **Types & Enums**: PascalCase (e.g., `Product`, `CartItem`).
- **Global Constants**: UPPER_SNAKE_CASE (e.g., `PRODUCTS`, `PARTNERS`).
